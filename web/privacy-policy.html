Privacy Policy for Plazo

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Plazo respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices when using the app.

Information We Collect

  • Account information such as your name and email address
  • Content you create in the app, such as tasks, exams, and related notes
  • Basic device and login-related information needed for app functionality
  • Authentication data processed through Firebase when you sign in with email or Google

How We Use Information

We use this information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Sync and display your app data
  • Provide login and account features
  • Improve app stability and user experience
  • Protect the app and prevent unauthorized access

Storage and Security

  • We use Firebase services to authenticate users and store account-related data
  • Some app data may be stored locally on your device for offline or faster access
  • We do not intentionally sell your personal information
  • We take reasonable steps to protect your data, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure

Third-Party Services

Plazo uses Firebase and Google Sign-In. These services may collect and process information according to their own privacy policies.

Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties except:

  • When required to provide the app's core functionality
  • When required by law
  • With service providers that help operate the app, such as Firebase

Data Deletion

You can delete your account from the app settings. When you delete your account, we remove or stop using account-related data as supported by the app and connected services.

Your Choices

  • Sign in using email or Google
  • Delete your account from the app settings
  • Stop using the app at any time

Children

Plazo is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will be made available with the app or on the app's website.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the app developer through the app or the support channel provided with the release.
