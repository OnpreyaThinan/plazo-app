Account Deletion Request for Plazo

Last updated: April 19, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your Plazo account and the personal data associated with it.

Important: Deleting your account will permanently remove your access to the app and account-related data, subject to legal and security retention requirements.

How to request deletion

  1. Open the Plazo app.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap Delete My Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion request.

If you cannot access the app, please contact the developer through the support channel listed on the app store page.

Data that will be deleted

  • Account profile information (for example name, email, profile image)
  • User content created in the app (tasks, exams, notes)
  • Account preferences and settings tied to your user account

Data that may be retained temporarily

  • Security and fraud-prevention records
  • Legal compliance or audit records when required by law
  • Short-term backups that are automatically removed after retention expires

Retention period

After a confirmed deletion request, account data is scheduled for removal as soon as reasonably possible. Some backup and security logs may remain for up to 90 days before automatic deletion, unless a longer retention period is legally required.

Contact

If you need help with account deletion, please use the support contact listed in the app store listing.
